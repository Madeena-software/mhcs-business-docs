02

Account activation

Initial payment gate

New registration

Create a pending member account

The member is signed in but cannot enter the active-member area yet.

Required action

Complete the initial payment / top-up

This activates both a new account and an upgraded walk-in account.

Payment result

Was payment successful?

NoKeep the account pending and allow payment retry.
YesActivate the member and open the dashboard.
Exception

Suspended account

Stop access and direct the member to support or an administrator.

YesConfirm the booking and reserve the shift.

Operator Core receives authorised attendance information. Walk-ins must be registered and paid first.
