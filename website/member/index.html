Account access

Decision

How was the account created?

02

Funding & first login

Keep business and personal points separate

B2B provisioning

Receive an active account and reserved points

The business pays centrally. Its annual value becomes reserved Madeena Points in the member's individual wallet.

Required action

Replace the temporary password

MHCS delivers credentials to the designated business contact outside Member Core. The imported member must change the random temporary password at first login.

Wallet rule

Which points may pay?

B2B bookingUse only its fully provisioned business-funded reservation.
B2C bookingUse only personal Madeena Points added by the member.
Exception

Suspended account

Stop access and direct the member to support or an administrator.

03

Booking & attendance

Assigned B2B or self-service B2C

Booking source

Is this booking B2B or B2C?

B2BThe business determines the examination, result option, location, date, and shift.
B2CThe member chooses an additional examination and pays with personal points.
Change control

Can the booking be changed?

A member cannot change a B2B booking. An MHCS administrator may act only on an official business request; B2C follows member booking rules.

Attendance result

Did the member attend?

No — B2BThe paid booking and quota remain consumed; attendance consequences belong to the business.
YesOperator Core receives the authorised paid booking.
Handoff

Attend the examination

Walk-ins and additional B2C bookings must still be registered and paid before operator confirmation.
