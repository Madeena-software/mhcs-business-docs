  1. The member books an examination, chooses a service, and pays.
  2. The operator confirms attendance, manages the queue, captures images, reviews the set, and submits it.
  3. The MHCS Platform accepts and processes the complete image set.
  4. The complete images become available to the member independently of AI and doctor review.
  5. If AI analysis was selected, the AI Service generates a report that the platform publishes automatically.
  6. If doctor review was selected, the doctor claims the study, reviews it, and submits a final report.
  7. The member can view each selected report independently before the journey completes.
